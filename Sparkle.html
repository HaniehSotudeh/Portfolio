Sparkle

  • UNC Charlotte
    Fall 2021
    Group project, Semester-long
    Instructor: Rachel Dickey
    Role: Ideation, Scripting, Fabrication, Drawings

Sparkle is a design based attempt to investigate computational thinking and design process step by step. Based on initiative studies on pattern formation and different variations of parameters of starfish in 2D environment, a final 3D Pavilion will be designed.

    Design Process

    The Design process evolved from a pattern study for a 2D model fabrication and a 3D form finding in the second step. The 2D study included investigating different parameters and their variation in the starfish pattern, the spines, and different elements in the star geometry like the number of arms and their proportions.
